Workreport Angst

Copyright (C) 2000 by Elliott Noel
(As seen in MathNews)

I had a pleasant workterm,
I wrote some funky stuff.
But now I'm done my workterm -
you'd think that'd be enough!
It seems they want a work report,
to show that I can write...
But I don't want to do one,
and I think that's alright.

My boss doesn't want to mark it,
he's much too busy for that.
I can't think of a topic
that isn't utter crap.
I sit here at my keyboard,
my brain begins to shrivel.
I could be learning something
but instead I'm typing drivel.

Why am I still in co-op,
do I really need employment?
Should I quit now while I can,
for the sake of life's enjoyment?
I think I'll just say "screw it",
and go do something else.
Perhaps this poem I have writ'
will save some mental health.
