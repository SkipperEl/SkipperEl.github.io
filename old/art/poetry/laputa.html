Laputa, Castle in the Sky

Copyright Elliott Noel, 1996

From an airship she falls down
Into a little mining town
Her jewel glowing bright she floats like air
Unaware of the powers it stores in there

First pirates come to steal the trinket
Then the government pursues the secret
Captured and held in a military base
Sheeta discovers her true name and race

Her evil compatriot activates the robot
Which seeks to protect the rightful despot
the base is engulfed in destruction and flame
Pazu and pirates return to rescue and reclaim

With Goliath airships they find Laputa
Pazu and Sheeta hitch a ride with mama
Military lackeys loot the place
Secret agents explore the inner spaces

Soon the power is in evil hands
Which would dominate and oppress the earth-bound lands
Giving the auto-destruct order by yell
Sheeta makes Laputa shed it's power-shell

Now but a harmless floating world
At peace long last - the storm is quelled
The island floats silently and beyond
Boot for all and danger gone.
