Curiosity
Copyright (C) 1998 by Elliott Noel


What if I noticed you - would you notice me?
would I catch your eye and hold your thought
would you pretend not to notice
or scan by without registering?

what if I looked and smiled, would you smile back?
with genuine radiance
would you smirk politely, not to be rude
or look away to focus on some distant object
pretending not to see?

what if I spoke to you, would you answer?
would you speak warmly and caringly
evasively, not wishing to be bothered
or with cold remark?

what if I held you, would you hold me?
in a firm embrace lasting an eternity
an uncomfortable hug wishing, I'd let go
or shirk, repulsed?

what if I kissed you, would you kiss me?
deeply, passionately, so as to lose awareness of the world around
a forced smile and an insincere peck
or would you simply push me away?

what if I told you I loved you, would you do the same?
honestly and truthfully, from the depth of your heart
would you lie to make me feel good
or dismiss me with disinterest?

what if?
Perhaps it is best not to know
and remain curious.
